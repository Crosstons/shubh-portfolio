## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience: recruiters/hiring managers scanning on desktops during work hours, developer peers browsing on various devices (often at night), and potential clients evaluating for freelance/contract work. Portfolio of Shubh Patel — software developer specializing in TypeScript, React, Next.js with 2+ years experience.

### Brand Personality
**Modern, minimal, futuristic** — Quiet confidence and technical mastery. Visitors should feel they're looking at someone who understands craft at a deep level. Not flashy, but unmistakably intentional.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark mode primary
- **Tone**: Precise, restrained futurism — clean geometry, purposeful negative space, subtle depth through surface hierarchy
- **Typography**: Bricolage Grotesque (display) + Figtree (body)
- **Color**: Warm ochre/amber accent (OKLCH hue ~80) on dark surfaces tinted toward the same hue. NOT cyan, purple, or blue.
- **Anti-references**: Generic developer portfolios with neon gradients, particle backgrounds, matrix rain, glassmorphism cards, cyan-on-dark palettes

### Design Principles
1. **Content over chrome** — Every visual element must serve the content. No decoration for its own sake.
2. **Quiet confidence** — The design should feel assured without shouting. Sophistication through restraint.
3. **Purposeful motion** — Animations communicate state and guide attention, never distract.
4. **Typographic hierarchy** — Text is the primary design tool. Strong contrasts in size and weight create structure.
5. **Futuristic grounding** — Modern and forward-looking, but grounded in usability. Not sci-fi theater.
